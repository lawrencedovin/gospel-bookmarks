Directions

Step1

Choose Design

Select your preferred bookmarks by the drop down menu which updates the preview of the bookmarks.

Step2

Print

After you have chosen your design click the “Print Bookmark” button and print the document on A4 paper.

Step3

Laminate

Take your printed out document and then laminate it to your nearest office supplies store. ie. Staples

Step4

Cut Out

Cut your laminated bookmark sheets to finish up. Feel free to print as many as you want. Enjoy!
